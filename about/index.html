About me.

I'm a Phd student working with the MEG Physics group MEG Physics group at the Institute of Neuroscience and Medicine (INM4), Forschungszentrum Juelich. I'm being supervised by Prof. N. J. Shah and Dr. Juergen Dammers. My main areas of interest are Magnetoencephalography (MEG), Electroencephalography (EEG), Connectivity analysis and Neuroimaging. My current work is focussed on analysing resting state connectivity in the human brain using MEG.
